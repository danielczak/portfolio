I am a visual artist and designer currently based in Berlin, who is highly passionate about turning borders into bridges through a creative practice that is generous and inclusive of diverse audiences.
I am doing so by developing visual languages that incorporate tokens of familiarity in order to accommodate viewers in highly curated environments. I strive the most under conditions in which I can research, conceptualize and problem- solve in order to mediate between cultures.
